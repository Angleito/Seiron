Assessment: Most complex pattern, generally well-distributed

Potential Issues Identified

  • 6-Star Asymmetry: Top-center star at 20% vs bottom-center at 80% creates vertical imbalance
  • 7-Star Edge Positioning: Middle-left/right stars at 20%/80% might be too close to edges
  • Vertical Spacing: Some configurations have uneven vertical distribution
  • Visual Balance: The positioning should feel naturally balanced within the circular orb

Recommended Improvements

  • 6-Star Fix: Adjust top-center to 25% and bottom-center to 75% for better symmetry
  • 7-Star Fix: Move middle-left/right stars to 25%/75% for better balance
  • Consistent Spacing: Ensure all stars are at least 20% from edges
  • Visual Testing: Test with actual Dragon Ball colors and gradients
